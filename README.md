# Car-Insurance-Claim-Report

## Overview

#### The Car Insurance & Claim Report Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze insurance policies, claim amounts, and customer demographics. It provides actionable insights for better decision-making in the insurance domain.

## Features

### KPI Cards:
#### 1.Total Policies
#### 2.Total Claim Amount
#### 3.Claim Frequency
#### 4.Average Claim Amount

## Interactive Slicers:
#### 1.Marital Status
#### 2.Gender
#### 3.Kids Driving

## Visualizations:
#### 1.Claim Amount by Car Use (Private vs Commercial).
#### 2.Claim Amount by Car Make.
#### 3.Claim Distribution by Coverage Zone.
#### 4.Claim Trends by Car Year.
#### 5.Claim Amount by Marital Status.
#### 6.Claim Amount by Education Level.

## Tools & Technologies
#### 1.Microsoft Power BI
#### 2.Data Modeling
#### 3.DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
#### 4.Data Visualization Techniques


## Insights Generated
#### High claim amounts observed in specific car makes like Ford and Chevrolet.

## How to Use
#### 1.Download the .pbix file.
#### 2.Open in Power BI Desktop.
#### 3.Use slicers to filter data dynamically.
#### 4.Explore visuals for insights.
#### 5.Urban and highly urban zones contribute the most to claims.
#### 6.Claim trends fluctuate significantly across years.
#### 7.Customer demographics impact claim frequency and amount.
