# Car-Insurance-Claim-Report

1. Overview

>The Car Insurance & Claim Report Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze insurance policies, claim amounts, and customer demographics. It provides actionable insights for better decision-making in the insurance domain.

2.Features

>> KPI Cards:
>Total Policies
>Total Claim Amount
>Claim Frequency
>Average Claim Amount

>>Interactive Slicers:
>Marital Status
>Gender
>Kids Driving

>>Visualizations:
>Claim Amount by Car Use (Private vs Commercial)
>Claim Amount by Car Make
>Claim Distribution by Coverage Zone
>Claim Trends by Car Year
>Claim Amount by Marital Status
>Claim Amount by Education Level

>>Tools & Technologies
>Microsoft Power BI
>Data Modeling
>DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
>Data Visualization Techniques
>>

3.Insights Generated
High claim amounts observed in specific car makes like Ford and Chevrolet

4.How to Use
a. Download the .pbix file
b. Open in Power BI Desktop
c. Use slicers to filter data dynamically
d. Explore visuals for insights
Urban and highly urban zones contribute the most to claims
Claim trends fluctuate significantly across years
Customer demographics impact claim frequency and amount
